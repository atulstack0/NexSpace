NexSpace
Spatial Office · Core Mechanic Prototype

You're an avatar in a top-down virtual office. You hear and are heard only by people inside your audio bubble — walk closer and a colleague fades in and pans toward you. Step into a room to hear everyone in it (and no one outside). Try the doors, the media wall, broadcast mode, and flip the same office into 3D — audio and positions never change.

🚶 WASD / arrows / click to move
🧊 Toggle 2D ↔ 3D (top-left)
📢 Broadcast to the whole floor
📺 Click the screen to play / pause
🚪 Knock on a closed room door
🎚️ Change your audio range
🎧 Headphones recommended — live spatial audio (soft synthesized "chatter") lets you hear the proximity effect. No mic, nothing recorded.
Self-contained · no install · no backend · no CDN. Mirrors the Phaser + Web Audio + view-agnostic-state architecture in the build plan (spec §6.1–6.5, §6.22–6.23).
